Hello and welcome to Career Term.*Career Term is a week-long event during winter break for sophomores to build career competencies.*Career Term targets sophomores so that they're better prepared for their job search by the time they graduate.*Career Term begins with a morning of service projects in the local community because students learn about themselves, their values, and the needs of the world around them when they serve others.*Many of the guest speakers at Career Term are Hendrix Alumni. Connecting Hendrix students with Hendrix alumni is one of Career Term's biggest benefits.*You will get a chance to learn and practice skills such as résumé writing and interviewing, personal branding, and professional communication, dress, etiquette, and networking.
